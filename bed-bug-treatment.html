About this category

Why homeowners and renters compare bed bug treatment options

Bed bug concerns are usually tied to sleeping areas, guest rooms, upholstery, or shared residential spaces. Because this category feels highly personal and interior-specific, many users want a clearer comparison path before contacting providers.

A service category page helps organize that process around the issue itself instead of sending the user into a generic search with no structure.
