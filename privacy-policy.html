Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Nestoria Pest Match may collect, use, and manage information submitted through the website.

Information We Collect

Nestoria Pest Match may collect information that users voluntarily submit through forms on the website. This may include name, phone number, email address, ZIP code, service category, property type, and any optional notes submitted in a request.

The website may also collect certain non-personal information such as browser type, device type, general usage patterns, referring pages, and interactions with the site.

How We Use Information

Information may be used to:

  • process or organize quote request submissions,
  • help connect homeowners with independent local service providers,
  • respond to general inquiries,
  • improve website functionality, usability, and site performance,
  • maintain records related to request activity and platform operations.

Sharing Information

Information submitted through the website may be shared with independent contractors or service providers for the purpose of helping users compare options or request quotes.

Nestoria Pest Match does not represent that any provider is employed by, controlled by, or directly part of this website. All contractors/providers are independent.

Cookies and Analytics

The website may use cookies, local storage, or similar technologies to support core site behavior, remember user preferences, improve navigation, and help evaluate general website usage.

Analytics or measurement tools may be used to better understand traffic patterns, content performance, and interaction with forms or navigation elements.

Data Retention

Information may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary for request handling, platform operations, recordkeeping, legal compliance, or performance monitoring.

Retention periods may vary depending on the nature of the data and the reason it was collected.

Your Choices

Users may choose not to submit certain information through the website, but some features—such as quote request functionality—may require basic contact and request details to operate as intended.

Users may also review browser settings to manage cookies and local data behavior, subject to how the site functions on specific devices or browsers.
